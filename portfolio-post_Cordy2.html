The Little Robot Returns

In 2013 we released Cordy 2, which was featured as Apples Editors' Choice, and named by Apple as Best of 2013. Google also named it one of the Top Android Games for 2013.

The story picks up after a long and harrowing journey, Cordy and Volt finally arrive at World 2, only to find it besieged by the evil Boogaloo. Players help Cordy and Volt as they set off to save the world from Boogaloo's evil Boogie Bots!

Cordy Gets a Some Powerful Friends

We wanted the sequel to Cordy to capture all the great qualities of the original while also expanding on the universe and gameplay. I always loved characters that gain new capabilities when paired with another character. We gave Cordy robots that attach to him, allowing him to smash through surfaces, magnetize to walls, run upsidedown, and of course fly with a jetpack!

Praise for Cordy 2

It isn’t often that a game manages to define a genre, but that’s exactly what Cordy 2 has done. From its gorgeous visual design to its lovable characters and perfect platforming, this fast-paced, colorful game cements itself with the likes of League of Evil and Rayman Jungle Run as a must- play classic.
