A Short Introduction

For the past ten years I have been creating interactive experiences for companies such as Disney, Microsoft, EA and Google. The projects include mobile games, educational training simulations, virtual reality experiences and theme park attractions. My goal is to design visually rich and magical products that audiences will find familiar yet completely new. I live in Menlo Park California with my wife Leslye and our daughter Lucy.

Artist

At a young age I became interested in the craftsmanship behind creating animated films. The life of an animator sketching away at a comfy drafting table seemed like a wonderful way to make a living. My central skill is the ability to quickly draw with expression to capture the essence of a concept. It is also my favorite pastime.

Designer

I love watching a design move from whiteboard to wireframe to interactive prototype as the initial concept is born. Finding simplicity in a design is often the most complex job. There is also a joy in conducting periodic user tests to prove out a concept, identifying which elements resonate with the audience.

Director

When leading teams of designers and artists, I like to think of the concept of "painting with people". I enjoy being responsible for the overall vision of a project, creating clear and inspiring goals, and encouraging teammates to hone and express their individual talents toward a shared goal.
